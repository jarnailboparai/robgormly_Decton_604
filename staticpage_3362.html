Dectons’ Core Values

Mission Statement

We strive to have our employees achieve their highest potential by providing meaningful employment for good people.  We are committed to workplace safety early in our sales process.  Client and employee satisfaction is a top priority for Decton, supplying only fully-qualified candidates in a timely manner, to meet clients’ needs.  Matching people to the environment they work in, is what allows Decton to provide uniquely qualified personnel.

Work Ethic
“Plan your work for today and every day, then work your plan.” – Margaret Thatcher

Customer Service
“To give real service you must add something that cannot be bought or measured with money; and that is sincerity and dignity.” – Douglas Adams
We are driven by a unique brand promise to Make It Happen! This means that we strive do whatever is necessary to consistently exceed your staffing needs.

Core Values:

CLIENT SERVICE
Our Clients’ needs drive our business.  We continuously seek to better understand those needs and offer
solutions that deliver value and create long-term relationships.

PEOPLE
We recognize that our strength and our competitive advantage is – and always will be – our people.  We are committed to an environment that attracts, motivates and recognizes high performance.

INTEGRITY
We work to build relationships based on trust by taking responsibility, acting ethically, and encouraging open and honest interaction.

TEAMWORK
We effectively collaborate, always looking for more efficient ways to serve our clients.

RESPECT
We work together to create a culture of inclusion built on trust, dignity and respect to all.
